Creamy Pulled Pork Soup

Description

This creamy soup can be made with leftover pulled pork. Cooks for total of 55 minutes including prep time of 10 minutes. Serves 6.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 3/4 cups cooked pulled pork
  • 2 tablespoons barbecue sauce
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley

Steps

  1. Combine milk and chicken broth in a large pot. Add pulled pork and barbecue sauce and stir. Stir in chili powder, black pepper, oregano, and salt. Let simmer over low heat, stirring occasionally, for 45 minutes.
  2. Divide into 6 bowls and top with parsley.
